About

This application uses advanced AI and machine learning techniques to analyze video footage for motion and speed detection. It can be used for various practical purposes like sports analysis, vehicle speed recognition, people running, animal and more. This type of analysis can be applied to various fields, including sports, security, automotive, medical diagnostics, and robotics. The goal is to determine the speed of moving entities, their trajectory, and potentially predict future movements.
